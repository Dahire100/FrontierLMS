# Portal Features

This document outlines the features and modules available within each portal of the FrontierLMS application.

## 1. Admin Portal
Comprehensive management and administrative tools for school operations.

- **Academics**: Manage classes, sections, subjects, and academic sessions.
- **Attendance**: Track student and staff attendance.
- **Bank Info**: Manage bank details for transactions.
- **Certificate**: Generate and manage student/staff certificates.
- **Classwork**: Manage daily classwork.
- **Communication**: Email, SMS, and notification systems.
- **Consent Letter**: Manage parent consent forms.
- **Disciplinary**: Handle disciplinary actions and records.
- **Download Center**: Upload and manage study materials and documents.
- **Examinations**: Schedule exams, manage question papers, and results.
- **Expense**: Track school expenses.
- **Fees Collection**: robust fee management, collection, and reporting.
- **Front CMS**: Manage frontend website content.
- **Front Office**: Reception, visitor logs, and phone call logs.
- **Homework**: Assign and monitor homework.
- **Hostel**: Manage hostels, rooms, and allocations.
- **Human Resource**: Staff management, payroll, and leaves.
- **Income**: Track school income.
- **Inventory**: Manage school inventory and assets.
- **Lesson Planner**: Plan lessons and curriculum.
- **Library**: Book management, issuing, and library rules.
- **Notice Board**: Post and manage notices.
- **Online Class**: Schedule and manage virtual classes.
- **Online Exam**: Conduct online examinations.
- **Primary Evaluation**: Evaluation report for primary students.
- **Question Paper**: Bank of question papers.
- **Report**: Generate various system reports.
- **Student Info**: Admission, detailed profiles, and promotion.
- **System Setting**: General system configuration.
- **Transport**: Vehicle, route, and driver management.
- **Wallet**: Manage student/parent wallets.

## 2. Student Portal
Self-service portal for students to access academic and personal information.

- **Academics**: View course details, syllabus, and curriculum.
- **Attendance**: View personal attendance records.
- **Class Timetable**: View weekly class schedule.
- **Communicate**: Message teachers and admins.
- **Documents**: Access and upload personal documents.
- **Download Center**: Download assignments and study materials.
- **Examinations**: View exam schedules and admit cards.
- **Fees**: View fee status and make online payments.
- **Homework**: View and submit homework assignments.
- **Hostel**: View hostel details (if applicable).
- **Leave Apply**: Apply for leaves.
- **Library**: Search books and view issued book history.
- **Notice Board**: View school notices.
- **Online Class**: Join scheduled online classes.
- **Online Exam**: Take online exams and quizzes.
- **Profile**: View and edit personal profile.
- **Report**: View report cards, progress reports, and semester-wise results.
- **Transport**: View transport route and vehicle details.
- **Wallet**: View wallet balance and transactions.

## 3. Teacher Portal
Tools for teachers to manage classes, students, and academic tasks.

- **Academics**: View assigned classes and subjects.
- **Attendance**: Mark and view student attendance.
- **Certificate**: Issue certificates? (View access).
- **Class Reports**: Generate class-specific reports.
- **Communicate**: Communicate with students and parents.
- **Consent Letter**: View consent status.
- **Disciplinary**: Report disciplinary issues.
- **Download Center**: Upload study materials for students.
- **Evaluation**: Student evaluation tools.
- **Events**: View school events.
- **Examinations**: input marks and viewing schedules.
- **Fees**: View student fee status (read-only typically).
- **Front Office**: Access basic front office info.
- **Homework**: Create and review homework.
- **Human Resource**: View personal profile and leave status.
- **Inventory**: Request inventory items.
- **Lesson Planner**: Create and track lesson plans.
- **Library**: Search library catalog.
- **Marks Entry**: Enter exam marks for students.
- **Notice Board**: View and post notices (if authorized).
- **Online Exam**: Manage online exams.
- **Student Info**: View student details.
- **Timetable**: View personal teaching schedule.

## 4. Parent Portal
Portal for guardians to monitor their child's progress and school activities.

- **Attendance**: partial daily/monthly attendance of the child.
- **Child Profile**: View child's personal details.
- **Class Timetable**: View child's class schedule.
- **Communicate**: Message teachers and school admin.
- **Consent Letter**: Approve/Sign consent letters.
- **Download Center**: Access downloadable resources for the child.
- **Exam Schedule**: View upcoming exams.
- **Examinations**: View exam results and history.
- **Fees**: Pay fees and view payment history.
- **Homework**: Monitor assigned and completed homework.
- **Hostel**: View hostel information (if applicable).
- **Leave**: Apply for leave on behalf of the child.
- **Library**: View books issued to the child.
- **Notice Board**: View important notices.
- **Online Class Links**: Access links for child's online classes.
- **Report**: View academic progress reports.
- **Transport**: Track transport details.

## 5. Super Admin Portal
High-level management for multi-school/SaaS configurations.

- **Billing**: Manage subscription billing and invoices.
- **Communication**: Broadcast messages across schools.
- **Enquiries**: Manage sales/school registration enquiries.
- **Institute Management**: Add, edit, and manage client schools.
- **Notifications**: System-wide notifications.
- **Platform Settings**: Global platform configurations.
- **SaaS Plans**: Manage subscription plans and pricing.
- **Support Tickets**: specific support ticket management.
